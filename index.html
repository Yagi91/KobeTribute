Kobe Bryant

1978-2020

Black Mamba

Home Tribute Timeline Gallery
Kobe Bryant was an exceptional basketball player and a true legend of the game. His talent, work ethic, and competitive spirit inspired countless people around the world. He will always be remembered for his incredible achievements on the court and his impact off of it. Rest in peace, Kobe.

Timeline

  • 1978: Born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
  • 1996: Drafted by the Charlotte Hornets
  • 2000: Drafted by the Los Angeles Lakers
  • 2008: Wins his first NBA championship
  • 2010: Wins his second NBA championship
  • 2012: Wins his third NBA championship
  • 2016: Wins his fourth NBA championship
  • 2020: Tragically dies in a helicopter crash
